## 📖 Full Description Template
SpendWise is your ultimate personal finance companion, designed to simplify expense tracking without compromising your privacy. Unlike other apps, SpendWise works entirely offline and processes your transactions right on your device.

Key Features:
✨ **Automatic SMS Parsing**: No more manual entries! SpendWise intelligently reads your bank/UPI SMS alerts and categorizes them automatically.
🔒 **Privacy First**: Your financial data is sensitive. That's why we never upload 
your transactions to any server. Everything stays on your phone.
📊 **Spending Insights**: See where your money goes with beautiful charts and category-wise breakdowns.
💰 **Budget Management**: Set spending limits for the month and get alerts when you're close to hitting them.
📅 **Search & Filter**: Find any transaction in seconds with powerful search and filtering options.

Note: SpendWise requires SMS permissions to provide automatic tracking features. We only read transactional messages and never share your data with third parties.
