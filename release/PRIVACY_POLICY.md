# Privacy Policy for SpendWise

**Last Updated:** April 17, 2026

SpendWise ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the SpendWise mobile application.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

### SMS Data (Sensitive Permission)
SpendWise requests access to your SMS messages (`READ_SMS` and `RECEIVE_SMS`) for the **sole purpose of automatic expense tracking**.
- **What we process:** We analyze incoming and existing text messages to identify financial transactions (e.g., bank alerts, UPI notifications, credit card debits).
- **Automated Processing:** Our algorithms extract the transaction amount, merchant name, and date to create an entry in your local ledger.
- **Privacy Guarantee:** All SMS processing happens **locally on your device**. We do not upload your raw SMS content or the parsed transaction details to any external servers.

### Financial Information
The app stores transaction data (amount, category, merchant) that you provide or that is parsed from SMS. This data is stored in a secure local database on your device.

## 2. Data Storage and Security
- **Local Storage:** SpendWise is an offline-first application. All your financial data and parsed info are stored on your device's internal storage.
- **No Cloud Sync:** By default, SpendWise does not transmit your financial data to the cloud. You are in full control of your data.

## 3. Data Sharing
We **do not** sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal or financial information to outside parties. Since all data is stored locally, we do not have access to your financial records.

## 4. Permissions
To provide its core features, the app may request the following:
- **SMS Permissions**: To automatically detect and import expenses.
- **Storage/Media**: To backup or export your transaction history (if initiated by you).

## 5. User Controls
- **Permission Revocation:** You can revoke SMS permissions at any time via your device settings. Note that this will disable automatic tracking.
- **Data Deletion:** You can delete specific transactions or clear the entire app database at any time from within the app settings.

## 6. Access for Children
Our app does not knowingly collect any personal identifiable information from children under the age of 13.

## 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
